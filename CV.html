My Curriculum Vitae

Born in 1970.

Associate editor of Geophysics and of Heliyon.

 

Positions Held:

October 2016 – present: CNRS Research Director (DR CNRS, first class), Laboratory of Mechanics and Acoustics, University of Aix-Marseille, Marseille, France.

October 2011 – October 2016: CNRS Research Director (DR CNRS, second class), Laboratory of Mechanics and Acoustics, University of Aix-Marseille, Marseille, France.

September 2007 – September 2012: Member of Institut universitaire de France ("junior member", non renewable).

January 2011 – September 2011: Professor of Geophysics, University of Toulouse, France.

January 2007 – December 2010: Director of the Laboratory of Modeling and Imaging in Geosciences, Pau, France, CNRS UMR 5212.

January 2005 – December 2006: Deputy Director of the Laboratory of Modeling and Imaging in Geosciences, Pau, France, CNRS UMR 5212.

September 2004 – December 2010: Professor of Geophysics, University of Pau, France.

December 2003 – August 2004: CNRS Researcher (CDD CNRS), University of Pau, France.

June 2000 – November 2003: Senior Research Fellow, Division of Geological and Planetary Sciences, California Institute of Technology (Caltech), USA.

September 1999 – June 2000: Research Associate, Department of Earth and Planetary Sciences, Harvard University, USA.

June 1999 – September 1999: Visitor, University of Mexico (UNAM), Mexico City, Mexico.

September 1998 – May 1999: Visitor, Department of Geological and Planetary Sciences, California Institute of Technology (Caltech), USA.

December 1997 – August 1999: Postdoctoral fellow, Department of Earth and Planetary Sciences, Harvard University, USA.

January 1994 – May 1997: Ph.D. student, Institut de Physique du Globe de Paris, France.
